Rimoxicam 1% Injection Solution – 1.5 ml

1.5 ml ampoule, 10 units per pack

Description

Rimoxicam is a non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) used to relieve pain and inflammation, available in injectable form.

Indications

  • Postoperative pain
  • Pain from injuries and burns
  • Osteoarthritis and rheumatic conditions
  • Vascular origin spasmodic pain

Administration

Administered intramuscularly or intravenously. Typically, adults receive 1–1.5 ml once per day. Repeat dose may be given after 24 hours, only by physician’s advice.

Contraindications

  • Severe hepatic or renal insufficiency
  • Active peptic ulcer disease
  • Severe heart failure
  • Allergy to NSAIDs or bronchial asthma
  • Third trimester of pregnancy and breastfeeding

Precautions

Prolonged use may irritate the gastrointestinal mucosa. Administer with sufficient fluid to reduce this risk.

Storage Conditions

Store at 5°C to 25°C in a dry, light-protected place out of children's reach. Shelf life: 3 years.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can it be mixed with oil?

No. The solution is intended strictly for IV or IM injection, without oil-based admixtures.

Is repeated injection allowed?

Yes, but not more than once daily and only as prescribed by a physician.

Is it suitable for children?

No. This medication is not approved for use in patients under 18 years of age.
