DIPHENHYDRAMINE 1% Injection Solution, 1ml

1ml ampoule, 10 ampoules per pack

Description

Diphenhydramine is a first-generation antihistamine with strong sedative and anti-allergic effects.

Indications

  • Allergic reactions (hives, hay fever, angioedema)
  • Anaphylactic reactions (as part of complex therapy)
  • Sleep disturbances
  • Sedation before medical procedures
  • Fever and excessive sweating

Administration

Intramuscularly or intravenously. Dosage is determined by a healthcare professional.

Contraindications

  • Hypersensitivity to diphenhydramine or other components
  • Newborns
  • Head injuries, epilepsy
  • Pregnancy and breastfeeding (only under doctor’s supervision)

Storage

Store in a dry, light-protected place at 5°C to 25°C. Keep out of reach of children. Shelf life: 3 years.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use diphenhydramine during pregnancy?

Only as prescribed and supervised by a doctor.

How should I store this medicine?

Store in a cool, dry, and dark place at 5–25°C.

What are the possible side effects?

Drowsiness, dizziness, dry mouth, and possible allergic reactions.
