Dexamethasone 4mg/ml Injection Solution

1 ml ampoule

Description

Dexamethasone is a potent anti-inflammatory and anti-allergic corticosteroid used in various inflammatory and immune-related conditions.

Indications

  • Allergic reactions
  • Bronchial asthma
  • Rheumatism and autoimmune diseases
  • Cerebral edema or stroke
  • Emergency hormonal therapy

Method of Administration

The drug is administered intramuscularly, intravenously, or as an infusion. Dosage is determined by a physician.

Contraindications

  • Allergy to dexamethasone
  • Active infectious diseases (tuberculosis, fungal infections)
  • Pregnancy (only under medical supervision and if necessary)

Storage Conditions

Store in a dry, dark place at a temperature between 15°C and 25°C. Keep out of reach of children. Shelf life: 2 years.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can dexamethasone be given to children?

Only with a doctor’s recommendation and with caution.

What are the methods of administration?

Intramuscularly, intravenously, or via infusion.

Are there any side effects?

Possible effects include weakened immunity, increased blood sugar levels, fatigue, and insomnia.
