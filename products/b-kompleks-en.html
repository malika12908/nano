B Complex 1ml Injectable Solution

1ml ampoule, 10 ampoules per pack

Description

B-complex vitamins are water-soluble and essential for supporting the nervous system, blood formation, and immune function.

Indications

  • Nervous system disorders (neuritis, neuralgia)
  • Anemia
  • Stress and physical fatigue
  • Vitamin B12 deficiency
  • Pregnancy (under doctor’s supervision)

How to Use

Intramuscular injection. 1ml per day or as directed by a physician.

Contraindications

  • Hypersensitivity to any B vitamins
  • Severe heart failure
  • Use in children only under medical supervision

Storage Conditions

Store in a dry, dark place between 5°C and 25°C. Keep out of reach of children. Shelf life: 2 years.
