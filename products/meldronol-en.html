Meldronol 500 mg/5 ml Injection Solution

Available in 5 ml ampoules, packaged in 10-piece boxes

Description

Meldronol (meldonium) is a metabolic agent that improves energy metabolism and blood flow. It is used to treat conditions related to ischemia, heart failure, and physical or mental overstrain.

Indications

  • Chronic heart failure
  • Physical and mental fatigue
  • Coronary heart disease
  • Recovery after surgery
  • Improved physical performance

Administration

The medication is administered intravenously or intramuscularly. The dosage and duration are determined by a physician. Typical dose: 500 mg (5 ml) once daily.

Contraindications

  • Hypersensitivity to meldonium
  • Increased intracranial pressure
  • Severe liver or kidney dysfunction
  • Pregnancy and lactation (unless prescribed)

Storage Conditions

Store in a dry, cool place at a temperature between 5°C and 25°C, away from direct sunlight. Keep out of reach of children. Shelf life: 3 years.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use Meldronol without a prescription?

No, it should only be used under medical supervision after consulting a doctor.

Is this product suitable for athletes?

Meldonium has been banned in professional sports. Consult with your healthcare provider if you are actively competing.

How long can I use Meldronol?

Duration depends on your condition. Always follow the physician’s guidance.
