Glucose Injection 40%

10ml ampoules, 10 units per pack

Description

Glucose 40% is a sterile hypertonic injection solution used as an energy source and to correct various metabolic disorders. The drug has detoxifying and rehydrating effects.

Indications

  • Hypoglycemia (low blood sugar)
  • Infectious diseases
  • Hepatitis and other liver disorders
  • Pulmonary and cerebral edema
  • Shock conditions
  • Dehydration (as part of complex therapy)

Dosage

Administered intravenously (bolus or infusion). Dosage is determined individually by a physician based on indications, patient age and clinical situation. The average single dose for adults is 20-50 ml of 40% solution.

Contraindications

  • Hyperglycemia
  • Diabetes mellitus (use with caution)
  • Cerebral and pulmonary edema
  • Acute left ventricular failure
  • Hyperosmolar coma

Storage Conditions

Store in a light-protected place at 5°C to 25°C. Protect from freezing. Shelf life - 3 years.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can glucose 40% be administered intramuscularly?

No, glucose 40% should only be administered intravenously. Intramuscular injection may cause tissue necrosis.

How often can glucose 40% be used?

Frequency of use is determined by a physician based on indications. For hypoglycemia it's administered once, for other conditions - according to the specialist's prescribed regimen.

Are there alternatives to this product?

Alternatives with the same active ingredient include: Glucose-Eskom, Glucose-Vial, Glucose-Biopharma. However, concentration and form may differ.
