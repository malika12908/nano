Piracetam 20%

Neuroprotective injection solution

Description

Piracetam 20% is a nootropic drug that improves brain function and protects neurons. It enhances cerebral circulation, strengthens memory and cognitive functions.

Indications

  • Memory impairment and attention deficit
  • Post-traumatic brain conditions
  • Neurocirculatory dystonia
  • Senile dementia

Dosage

Use only as prescribed by a physician. Standard doses range from 800 mg to 2400 mg divided into 2-3 daily administrations.

Frequently Asked Questions

How soon will I feel Piracetam working?

The effects of Piracetam vary between individuals. Some people notice effects after the first dose, while others may need several weeks of regular use to perceive differences.

What medications should not be taken with Piracetam?

Caution should be exercised when combining Piracetam with blood-thinning medications (warfarin, aspirin). Always consult your physician before combining with other medications.
