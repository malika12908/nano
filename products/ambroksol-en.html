AMBROXOL 15mg/2ml Injection Solution

2ml ampoule

Description

Ambroxol is a mucolytic agent that thins mucus and facilitates easier coughing. It helps clear mucus from the respiratory tract.

Indications

  • Bronchitis and pneumonia
  • Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD)
  • Asthma and conditions involving mucus and coughing

Dosage & Administration

This product is administered intramuscularly, intravenously, or by infusion. Dosage should be prescribed by a physician based on individual needs.

Contraindications

  • Allergy to ambroxol
  • First trimester of pregnancy
  • Use with caution in heart or liver diseases

Storage

Store in a dry, dark place at 15°C to 25°C. Keep out of reach of children. Shelf life: 3 years.
