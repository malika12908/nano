About Us

NANO Pharmaceuticals is one of the leading pharmaceutical companies in Uzbekistan, specializing in the production of modern medicines, conducting scientific research, and developing innovative solutions in the field of pharmaceuticals. Founded in 2019, our company quickly gained trust thanks to the high quality of its products.

Our core values are commitment to human health, high quality, safety, and innovation. All products are manufactured in state-of-the-art laboratories in accordance with international ISO and GMP standards.

Our team consists of over 100 professionals, including qualified pharmacists, biologists, chemists, and engineers who play a key role in creating new drugs using modern technologies.

Building on its scientific potential, NANO Pharmaceuticals has established an innovative research center where new drugs are developed to treat various diseases.

Our clients include public and private clinics, pharmacy chains, and distributors. We aim to build long-term cooperation with each partner.

The company also embraces social responsibility: we support healthcare charity projects and run initiatives to raise medical awareness among the population.

In the future, NANO Pharmaceuticals aims to further develop Uzbekistan's pharmaceutical industry and enter the international market. Our headquarters is located in Tashkent.
