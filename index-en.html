About Us

NANO Pharmaceuticals is one of Uzbekistan's leading pharmaceutical companies, specializing in the production of modern medicines, scientific research, and innovative pharmaceutical solutions. Established in 2019, our company quickly gained significant attention in the local market with reliable, high-quality products.

Our core values are commitment to human health, quality, safety, and innovation. Each product is manufactured in modern laboratories complying with international ISO and GMP standards.

Our team includes over 100 specialists including pharmacists, biologists, chemists, and engineers who play crucial roles in developing new products using modern technologies.

NANO Pharmaceuticals has established an innovative research center developing medications for various diseases.

Our clients include government and private hospitals, pharmacy chains, and distributors. We value long-term, effective partnerships.

We take social responsibility seriously, supporting healthcare charity projects and initiatives to improve medical literacy.

NANO Pharmaceuticals aims to advance Uzbekistan's pharmaceutical industry and expand internationally. Our headquarters are located in Tashkent.
